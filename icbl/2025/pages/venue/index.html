Suggested Accommodations

Participants can select an accommodation from a wide price range of hotels near the venue. Since the venue is next to the Saphan Taksin BTS Skytrain station, participants may choose any hotel with access to a nearby BTS Skytrain station. Here are a few suggestions:


Within walking distance (Bangrak Area):

This area is an old town style. It has many well-known local street foods along the main road. Across the river is a big shopping mall named ICON SIAM, and participants can take public boat rides to Chinatown or many world-renowned attractions such as the Palace, the Temple of the Dawn, or Asiatique.

  • Shangri-La Bangkok Hotel
    A luxurious hotel right at the conference venue by the Chaophraya River. The hotel offers a great view of the river and is located next to the Saphan Taksin BTS Skytrain station. It is also right across a stylish mall, ICON SIAM, where participants can take a free shuttle boat from the hotel to spend their free time there. > Map

  • Centre Point Plus Hotel Silom
    A four-star hotel near the Shangri-La Hotel (250 m away). It is also near the Saphan Taksin BTS Skytrain station (180 m away). > Map

  • Bossotel Inn Bangkok
    A three-star hotel near the Shangri-La Hotel (140 m away). The Saphan Taksin BTS Skytrain station is 450 m away. > Map



One Skytrain station away (Surasak Station Area):

This area has a street (Sathon 11 Alley) with many well-known local restaurants.

  • SAM E. Hotel Bangkok Sathorn
    A 3.5-star hotel located 1.1 km away from the venue. Instead of walking, participants can also choose to ride the BTS Skytrain for one stop. It is an affordable hotel next to the Surasak BTS station with a 7/11 convenience store inside the hotel. > Map

  • Mode Sathorn Hotel Bangkok
    A luxurious four-star hotel located right next to Surasak BTS station. It is 1.3 km from the venue if walking > Map

  • Eastin Grand Hotel Sathorn
    A luxurious four-star hotel located right next to Surasak BTS station. It is 1.2 km away from the venue if walking. > Map
